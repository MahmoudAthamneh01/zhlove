Q

Why can't I connect to online games?

Common Troubleshooting

Quick fixes for common issues

Game crashes on startup

Try running the game as administrator and update your graphics drivers.

Can't see other players in multiplayer

Check your firewall settings and ensure the game is allowed through Windows Defender.

Low FPS or lag

Lower the graphics settings and close unnecessary background programs.

Sound issues

Update your audio drivers and check the in-game audio settings.
